This is a recipe for a chicken urry. It is a classic English food originating from India.

Ingredients

  • 500g Basmati Rice
  • 32oz Boneless Skinless Chicken Thighs - diced
  • 16oz Curry Sauce
  • Assorted vegetables cut into large chunks
  • Naan bread
  1. Cook rice in rice cooker
  2. Sear the chicken
  3. Add the vegetables and curry sauce
  4. Warm the naan bread
